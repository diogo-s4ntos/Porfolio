    Get to know me!

    Hello everyone, I'm Diogo Santos, a web development enthusiast based in Lisbon. Currently immersing myself in the vast universe of frontend development, I'm passionate about creating incredible and interactive interfaces. While I'm just starting my journey in this field, I also have a some understanding of backend, particularly in database modeling and manipulation. My approach combines the creativity required for frontend work with the structured logic demanded by backend, resulting in robust and visually appealing web projects. After hours, I will find myself soaking up Lisbon's vibrant scenes with my girlfriend and friends. Enjoy meals together and stroll through the historic streets of Lisbon. I also have a deep love for film, and on calmer days you'll find me relaxing in front of the computer, exploring new technology and refining my skills. This mix of tastes not only shapes my personal life, but also influences my approach to creating unique and user-friendly web experiences.
